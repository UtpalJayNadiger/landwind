Frequently asked questions

TCR community edition is a whatsapp group where GPP (General Prep) strength and conditioning programs are shared every week. There are separate programs for Batsmen & Spinners, Fast Bowlers and Recreational cricketers. These programs are shared without support/coaching, reference videos or cues. These can be availed under the TCR pro plan. Community support is always available across all our social media accounts. Feel free to ping us there if you have any questions and we will do our best to answer your queries through the content we post.

TCR pro is our paid product offering for cricketers. Players who enroll under this plan can get access to a dedicated 1:1 channel with a TCR coach for queries, support and form checks. Our response times are typically in hours. We have coaches in all timezones and try to ensure quick and prompt response times.

TCR is for anyone who plays cricket or has played it in the past. Both our pro plans and community plans cater to professional cricketers who are currently playing competitively - we have specialists who have trained international fast bowlers, batsmen and spinners as part of the team. TCR can also be used by recreational cricketers who want to train like their favourite player and get back in shape. If you’re in either of the buckets above, we’d recommend that you get a taster for our programs through the community edition and then decide if you want to continue.

We train people of all abilities. Under confident and just starting off? We got you. Trained for 8 years and looking to optimize a few parts of your program? We got you too.
